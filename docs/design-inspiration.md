# VexDeals — Design Inspiration (from culture-circle.com)

Running notes of design/UX ideas to adapt into VexDeals (in our teal `#009fb7` +
yellow `#ffc107` premium theme — NOT a black/white copy). Source: Culture Circle
homepage screenshots.

## Look & feel (principles)
1. **Minimal high-contrast** — generous whitespace, clean bg, bold text. Premium = less clutter.
2. **Bold mixed-weight display typography** — big outlined wordmarks (e.g. "DROPS"), mixed bold/outline logo.
3. **Graph-paper / grid background texture** — subtle grid lines behind sections. Biggest "fresh/modern" trick, very low effort.
4. **One prominent CTA** — single bold pill (their "CLAIM YOUR FREE 999"). Don't scatter buttons.

## Standout sections / animations
5. **3D perspective product coverflow** — products float on a perspective grid floor, center large, sides angled, soft shadows, auto-rotate + arrows. The "wow" piece.
6. **Bento grid categories** — varied tile sizes, bold label over lifestyle image (SNEAKERS big, others small).
7. **Shop the Look ("Get this look")** — a curated outfit: model wearing the look + the individual products beside it + big CTA "Get this look". Great for bundles/cross-sell.
8. **Brand wall** — "Global Brands at the Lowest Prices": circular brand-logo chips (row + 2-row grid) + "View All Brands" outlined button.
9. **Tab switchers** — same section, filters (UNISEX / MEN / WOMEN).
10. **Marquee ticker** — running strip (we already have this).
11. **Editorial full-bleed hero** + left/right carousel arrows + "Shop Now" glass button.

## Footer
12. **Premium black footer** — pill quick-links row (What's New, SALE, Sneakers…) + SEO link columns (Most Viewed / Top 50 / Know More / Contact Us) with address + WhatsApp support.

## Product cards
13. Clean card: image on light bg, wishlist heart, name, price, black "Explore" button.
